IQ - Intelligence of a Mag. Affects the level of Shifta, Deband, and Resta from triggers (base level 1, +1 level for each 40 IQ rounded down)

### Mag同步率规则:

* +: 基础值+阶梯增幅 
* -: 只有阶梯增幅 
* 无: 忽略同步率
